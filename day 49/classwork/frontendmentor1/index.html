Mercedes

Mercedes-Benz commonly referred to as Mercedes
and sometimes as Benz, is a German luxury
and commercial vehicle automotive
brand established in 1926.









BMW

Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, commonly abbreviated to BMW
is a German multinational manufacturer of luxury
vehicles and motorcycles headquartered in Munich,
Bavaria, Germany.








Lamborghini

Automobili Lamborghini S.p.A.
is an Italian manufacturer of luxury sports
cars and SUVs based in Sant'Agata Bolognese.
The company is owned by the Volkswagen Group
through its subsidiary Audi.
